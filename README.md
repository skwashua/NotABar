# NotABar
In the State of Utah, eating establishments that serve alchohol are required to have signage stating whether they are a bar or restaurant.

Unfortunately, everyone outside of the State of Utah doesn't get this luxury.

Don't put your your family at risk and avoid the potential embarrassment.

Not A Bar is here to help!

Learn more about the law: http://bit.ly/NotABarGitHub
